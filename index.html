Hello! I am a masters student in Computer Engineering at Arizona State University. My interests lie in the broad domain of Machine Learning and Signal Processing. I am interested in working on problems that leverage Machine Learning to solve real life problems.

I graduated with a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Guwahati, Assam, India in 2023. I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. I did my bachelor thesis on the topic of In-depth Analysis of Seizure Types using Deep Learning at IIIT Guwahati where I was advised by Prof. Shovan Barma .

I also interned at IISc Bangalore in the neurooscillations lab as a part of the Summer Research Fellowship program from where I developed an interest in the application of engineering to the biomedical field. Thereafter, I also got selected for the Google Research week where I had the oppurtunity to listen to amazing researchers on their work in the field of Machine Learning.
After my bachelors I worked for a while at ZS Associates as a Business Technology Solutions Associate where I worked on sales data of medical representatives to generate valuable insights using technologies like SQL and Excel.

Currently I am extensively learning technologies like PyTorch and strengthening my fundamentals and working on personal projects, which you can check out on my Github For more details, check my Resume or hit me up on my email.
